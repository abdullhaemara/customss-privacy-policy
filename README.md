## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Custom Clearance** ("التخليص الجمركي") (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. By accessing or using the App, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use the App:

### 2.1 Personal Information

When you register for an account or use certain features, we may collect:

- **Name** (first and last name)
- **Email address**
- **Phone number**
- **Profile picture** (if provided)
- **Account credentials** (encrypted and securely stored)

### 2.2 Location Data

- We collect your **device location** data to provide location-based services, including finding nearby customs clearance service providers and displaying relevant services based on your geographic area.
- Location data is collected only when you grant permission and may be collected in the foreground while you use the App.

### 2.3 Order and Transaction Information

- Details about **customs clearance orders** you create or manage.
- **Service requests** and their status.
- **Wallet transactions** and payment-related information.

### 2.4 Communication Data

- **Chat messages** exchanged between clients and service providers within the App.
- **Notifications** sent to and received by your device.

### 2.5 Device and Usage Information

- **Device type**, operating system, and version.
- **Unique device identifiers**.
- **App usage data**, including pages visited, features used, and interaction patterns.
- **Crash reports and performance data** to improve App stability.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Account Management:** To create, maintain, and manage your account.
- **Service Delivery:** To connect clients with customs clearance service providers and facilitate order management.
- **Communication:** To enable in-app messaging between users and to send push notifications about order updates, promotions, and important announcements.
- **Location Services:** To display relevant services and providers based on your location.
- **Payment Processing:** To process wallet transactions and manage financial records within the App.
- **App Improvement:** To analyze usage patterns, diagnose technical issues, and improve the App's performance and user experience.
- **Legal Compliance:** To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
- **Safety and Security:** To protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and other security threats.

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, government agencies).

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored on secure servers using industry-standard encryption and security measures.
- We use **Firebase Authentication** for secure user login and account management.
- All data transmissions between the App and our servers are encrypted using **TLS/SSL** protocols.
- Chat messages and personal data are stored securely and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
- Despite our security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
